Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ZipPix handles information when you use our website and browser-based image tools.

1. Image files

ZipPix is designed to process images locally in your web browser. When you select a file, the file is processed on your device and is not uploaded to, transmitted to, or stored on our servers. We do not access the content of your images.

2. Information we collect

We may collect limited, non-identifying technical information needed to operate and improve the site, such as browser type, device category, pages viewed, and approximate usage metrics. We do not require an account or collect a name, address, or payment information to use the image compressor.

3. Cookies and analytics

We may use essential cookies to remember basic site preferences and privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect preferences, but not the local image-processing function.

4. How we use information

We use limited technical information to maintain security, understand site performance, diagnose issues, and improve the service. We do not sell your personal information or use selected images for advertising.

5. Service providers

We may use trusted service providers for hosting, analytics, and site delivery. They may process limited technical data only as needed to provide their services and under contractual confidentiality obligations.

6. Data retention

Because image files are processed locally, we do not retain them. Technical logs and aggregate analytics are kept only for as long as reasonably necessary for security and service improvement.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or object to processing of personal data. To make a request or ask a privacy question, email privacy@zippix.tools.

8. Updates

We may revise this policy when our practices or legal obligations change. We will post the updated version and revise the date at the top of this page.
