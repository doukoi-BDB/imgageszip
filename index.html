Questions, answered.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. Image compression happens in your browser. Your files are not uploaded or stored by ZipPix.

Which image formats can I compress?

You can add JPG, PNG, and WebP files. Choose WebP when you want the smallest modern web format.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. ZipPix works in current mobile and desktop browsers.
